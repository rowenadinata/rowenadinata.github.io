Hello! Nice to meet you

My name is Rowena Dinata, I'm an Indonesia based web designer & front-end developer. Focusing on designing simple, clean and beauty user experiences.

About Me

I was born in 1987, and graduated from design college in 2010. Basically at the first time i'm just another graphic designer. Then in August 2013, I transformed my role into a web designer and front-end developer. As why I’m very much familiar with the 'art' things but also fascinated with the information technology.

- DESIGN -

Graphic design is one of my expertise, as I mentioned at the top of this page. I used to be a graphic designer and the experiences very helpful to me when create a website. Design is an essential and be part of element ingredients for successful website or any other media to deliver what client wants.

- FRONT-END TECHNOLOGY -

Nowadays the second most important for web designer is a basic coding skills. It would be very helpful when you build the logic of a website and would accelerate the work itself.

- UX & UI -

User Experience and User Interface Design is a new golden field. Without this thing, website can be confusing and not friendly. With the new paradigm that consumer or user would be a center of the product, then the website will be better.
